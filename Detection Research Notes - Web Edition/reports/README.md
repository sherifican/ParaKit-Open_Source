1. **[Detection Cleanup & Results](1-detection-cleanup-and-results.md)** — how ParaKit cleans up the detector output, and the measured before/after on held-out songs.

3. **[Literature Review & Findings](3-literature-review-and-findings.md)** — 31 papers cross-checked against hands-on testing.
